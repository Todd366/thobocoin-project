## 🔥 Key Features

### Smart Contracts (Professional Grade)
- ✅ **THoBoCoin**: BEP-20 token, 1B supply, 0% tax, staking, deflationary

- ✅ **THoBoGovernance**: Community voting with 7-day periods, 10% quorum
